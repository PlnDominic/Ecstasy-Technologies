## IMPORTANT NOTES

### Content Writing Tips:
```
✅ Write for humans, not just search engines
✅ Use short paragraphs (3-4 sentences)
✅ Include bullet points and lists
✅ Add images and screenshots
✅ Link to your services
✅ End with clear CTA
✅ Proofread everything
```
